# Getting Started with AI Thinking

## Welcome!

This guide will help you set up everything you need for CAI1001C: Artificial Intelligence Thinking.

---

## Step 1: Create a Google Account (If You Don't Have One)

1. Go to [accounts.google.com](https://accounts.google.com)
2. Click "Create account"
3. Follow the prompts
4. **Remember your login credentials!**

---

## Step 2: Access Google Colab

Google Colab is our cloud-based Python environment. Everything runs in your web browser - no installation needed!

1. Go to [colab.research.google.com](https://colab.research.google.com/)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. You should see the Colab welcome screen

### Test Your Setup

Let's make sure everything works:

1. Click **File ‚Üí New Notebook**
2. In the first cell, type: `print("Hello AI!")`
3. Press **Shift + Enter** (or click the Play button)
4. You should see `Hello AI!` appear below the cell

**Congratulations!** You're ready to code.

---

## Step 3: Access Course Materials from GitHub

All course materials are in our GitHub repository. Here's how to open notebooks:

### Method 1: Open Directly in Colab (Recommended)

1. Go to [colab.research.google.com](https://colab.research.google.com/)
2. Click **File ‚Üí Open Notebook**
3. Click the **GitHub** tab
4. Enter repository URL
5. Browse to the chapter you need
6. Click on the notebook to open it

### Method 2: Download and Upload

1. Go to the GitHub repository in your web browser
2. Navigate to the notebook file (`.ipynb`)
3. Click on the file, then click **Raw**
4. Right-click ‚Üí Save As ‚Üí Save to your computer
5. In Colab: **File ‚Üí Upload Notebook**
6. Select the file you downloaded

---

## Step 4: Saving Your Work

**IMPORTANT:** Colab notebooks are NOT automatically saved to your Google Drive unless you save them manually.

### To Save Your Work:

1. **File ‚Üí Save a copy in Drive**
2. This creates a copy in your Google Drive under "Colab Notebooks" folder
3. Rename it appropriately (e.g., "GP01-Meet-Your-AI-Assistant-YourName")
