# Discussion Rubric
**10 points per discussion | 5 Discussions total**

## Format

- **Initial Post:** Thursday 11:59 PM (5 points)
  - 150-200 words
  - Ethical analysis
  - Specific examples
  - Apply course concepts

- **Two Peer Replies:** Friday 11:59 PM (2.5 points each)
  - 75+ words each
  - Substantive engagement
  - Build on ideas
  - Ask questions

## Tips

✅ Use specific examples  
✅ Apply course concepts  
✅ Consider multiple perspectives  
✅ Engage meaningfully with peers  
❌ Don't just say "I agree"  
❌ Don't wait until last minute
